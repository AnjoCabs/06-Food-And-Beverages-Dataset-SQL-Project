# 06-Food-And-Beverages-Dataset-SQL-Project

# 📈 Sales Performance & Business KPI Analysis Using SQL

## 📖 Project Overview

This project demonstrates how SQL can be used to analyze sales performance and business key performance indicators (KPIs) for a wholesale and retail distribution company. The analysis focuses on revenue generation, product performance, sales channels, supervisor performance, customer purchasing behavior, and seasonal sales trends.

Using MySQL, this project transforms transactional sales data into actionable business insights that support strategic planning, sales optimization, inventory management, and revenue growth.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

This project aims to answer critical business questions, including:

- What is the company's overall sales performance?
- Which products contribute the most revenue?
- Which supervisors generate the highest sales?
- How do sales fluctuate throughout the year?
- Which sales channels drive the most revenue?
- How can the company increase profitability and customer lifetime value?

# 📊 Business Analysis

## 📌 KPI Performance Analysis

The overall business performance reveals several important operational metrics.

### Key Performance Indicators

| KPI | Value |
|------|-------:|
| Total Revenue | **₱20.37 Million** |
| Total Orders | **246,000** |
| Average Order Value (AOV) | **₱411** |
| Average Units per Transaction | **~28 Units** |

### Key Findings

- Core staple products remain the primary revenue drivers.
- Customers continue to purchase in bulk, indicating strong wholesale demand.
- Online sales contribute only **14%** of total revenue.
- Wholesale customers generate higher Average Order Values than online customers.

### Business Recommendation

To reduce dependence on traditional channels, the company should:

- Increase digital sales penetration.
- Improve Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) through product bundles.
- Expand cross-selling initiatives.
- Improve omnichannel customer experience.

---

# 🛒 Product Revenue Analysis

The analysis identifies the company's highest-performing products and categories.

### Key Findings

- Flour, cooking oils, and yeast generate **over 60% of total company revenue**.
- Staple products remain the foundation of business profitability.
- Premium products such as luxury wines and energy drinks contribute relatively little revenue.
- Seasonal products like **Lollipop Twirls** generate occasional sales spikes.

### Business Recommendation

To improve profitability:

- Strengthen supply chain planning for high-demand products.
- Implement cross-selling between staple and premium products.
- Optimize inventory allocation.
- Review underperforming SKUs for possible rationalization.
- Improve Gross Margin Return on Investment (GMROI).

# 👨‍💼 Sales Supervisor Performance Analysis

Sales performance varies significantly across supervisors.

### Revenue Contribution

| Supervisor | Revenue Contribution |
|------------|---------------------:|
| Emmanuel | **37.5%** |
| Noel | **34.7%** |
| Lara | **17.3%** |
| Anna | **8.9%** |
| Marco | **3.8%** |

### Key Findings

- Emmanuel and Noel generate approximately **72% of total company revenue**.
- Revenue generation is heavily concentrated among two supervisors.
- Significant performance gaps exist across the sales organization.

### Business Recommendation

Improve salesforce productivity by:

- Documenting best practices from top-performing supervisors.
- Creating structured sales coaching programs.
- Implementing Performance Improvement Plans (PIPs).
- Reviewing territory assignments.
- Expanding strategic account management.
- Introducing performance-based incentive programs.

---

# 📅 Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

Revenue demonstrates clear seasonality throughout the year.

### Key Findings

- Lowest monthly revenue occurs in **January (₱0.80M)**.
- Highest revenue occurs in **October (₱2.36M)**.
- Approximately **40% of annual revenue** is generated during **September through December**.

### Business Recommendation

During low-demand periods:

- Launch promotional campaigns.
- Offer loyalty rewards.
- Introduce product bundles.
- Encourage repeat purchases.

During peak seasons:

- Increase inventory levels.
- Improve warehouse capacity.
- Apply premium pricing where appropriate.
- Focus on upselling high-margin products.
